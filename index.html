Developh Library

The Developh Library is a physicalWIP and digital collection of books and texts curated by Filipinos thinking critically about technology, design, and their intersections.

While the Philippine is a prime case study for technology's adverse effects, there's a dearth of access to both public libraries and publications & printed matter in the country
